All About
Meghan O'Connor

I am a marathon runner, dog mom, teacher, and a UX designer. As you can see I bring an overwhelming sense of dedication and planning to every aspect of my life. Choosing to run 5 marathons and over half a dozen half marathons took careful planning, dedication and persistence. This can also be seen in my career path as I have over a decade worth of experience in education and a strong passion for accessibility. I have spent my career educating children with various ethic, economic and cognitive backgrounds. The past 3 years I have been working with students with autism and have seen their own personal version of dedication and persistence with the challenges they face daily. I want to help improve their lives by helping designs become more intuitive, simple and engaging for children of all cognitive levels. My background in education has taught me how to multitask, plan with the future in mind, set both large and small goals, the importance of staying organized, and how to manage teams. I am confident that I can provide a design team a new way of thinking and help improve user experience for all ages and abilities.
